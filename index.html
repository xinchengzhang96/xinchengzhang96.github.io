Research

I am interested in solvable models in probability, in particular, models in the KPZ universality class like the totally asymmetric exclusion process and the exponential last passage percolation models. I am also interested in their common limiting object, the KPZ fixed point. One particular focus of my recent work is to understand the KPZ fixed point from a PDE perspective.
